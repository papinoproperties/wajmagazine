Privacy Policy

WAJ Magazine / We Are Jersey ENT LLC — wajmagazine.com

Last Updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how WAJ Magazine ("WAJ," "we," "us," or "our"), operated by We Are Jersey ENT LLC, collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit wajmagazine.com (the "Site"), make purchases, submit content, or interact with our services. By using the Site, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Make a purchase through our shop (name, email, shipping address, payment information)
  • Submit a blog post or contact form (name, email, content you provide)
  • Sign up for our email newsletter (email address)
  • Submit an advertising or partnership inquiry (name, email, business name, message)
  • Create an account or register for an event

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • IP address and general location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit and time spent on those pages
  • Referring website or source
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our Site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some Site functionality. We may use analytics tools such as Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill orders (digital downloads and physical print copies)
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Send newsletters and marketing communications (only if you opt in)
  • Review and respond to blog post submissions and partnership inquiries
  • Improve the Site's content, design, and functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and protect the security of our Site

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Payment Processing

All payment transactions on this Site are processed securely by Stripe. WAJ Magazine does not store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment information on our servers. Stripe's use of your payment data is governed by their own privacy policy, available at stripe.com/privacy.

For physical print orders, we collect your shipping address solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order. We do not share this with third parties except the shipping carrier required to deliver your order.

4. Email Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter or opt in to marketing communications, you may receive emails about new issues, blog posts, events, and promotions. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email or by contacting us directly.

Transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping notifications) are sent regardless of marketing preferences as they are required to fulfill your purchase.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers only as necessary to operate our business, including:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Netlify — website hosting and form handling
  • Email marketing platforms (e.g., Mailchimp) — newsletter delivery
  • Shipping carriers — fulfillment of physical orders
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) — site performance analysis

We may also disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of WAJ Magazine or others.

6. Digital Downloads

After purchasing a digital magazine issue, you will receive a download link via email. Digital files are for personal, non-commercial use only. Redistribution, resale, or reproduction of digital downloads is prohibited without written permission from WAJ Magazine.

7. User-Submitted Content

When you submit a blog post or article for consideration, you grant WAJ Magazine a non-exclusive license to publish, edit, and distribute that content on our Site and related platforms if approved. You retain ownership of your original work. We will credit you as the author of any published content.

Submitted content is reviewed by our editorial team. Submission does not guarantee publication.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Order records are retained for a minimum of 7 years for tax and accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us (see Section 10).

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (for EU/UK residents under GDPR)

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt out of its sale (we do not sell personal information).

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to exercise your data rights, or need to report a privacy concern, please contact us:

  • Email: magazine@wearejerseyent.com
  • Website: wajmagazine.com/contact
  • Company: We Are Jersey ENT LLC, New Jersey, USA

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
