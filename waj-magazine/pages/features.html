WAJ Magazine Features

GET
FEATURED

WAJ Magazine has been amplifying the voices of creatives, entrepreneurs, athletes, and community leaders since 2014. We believe every story deserves to be told — and we choose our features based on merit, not money.

Merit-Based Selection — No Payment Required

How It Works

01

Submit Your Story

Fill out the form below with your background, your story, and why you believe your voice belongs in WAJ Magazine.

02

Editorial Review

Our team reviews every submission personally. We look at your story, your impact on the community, and your cultural significance.

03

We Reach Out

If selected, a member of our editorial team will contact you within 30 days to discuss scheduling, photos, and your feature.

04

Your Story, Published

Your feature goes live in WAJ Magazine — print and digital — reaching our audience of creatives, tastemakers, and culture lovers.

Who We Feature

We have featured over 500 voices across music, sports, fashion, food, business, and community activism. If you're doing something worth talking about in New Jersey and beyond, we want to hear from you.

Artists & Musicians

Recording artists, producers, DJs, visual artists, photographers, and creative directors making an impact in their field.

Entrepreneurs & Business Owners

Founders, CEOs, and small business owners building something meaningful in New Jersey and beyond.

Athletes & Sports Figures

Professional and amateur athletes, coaches, and sports personalities with a story bigger than the game.

Community Leaders

Activists, nonprofit founders, educators, and changemakers creating real impact in their communities.

Fashion & Lifestyle

Designers, stylists, influencers, and tastemakers shaping culture through personal expression and creativity.

Food & Hospitality

Chefs, restaurateurs, culinary innovators, and food personalities building dining culture in the Garden State.

Submit for Consideration

Tell us your story. Our editorial team reads every submission and selects features based entirely on merit — your impact, your story, and your connection to the culture we cover. There is no fee to submit or to be featured.

Submission Received!

Thank you for reaching out. Our editorial team will review your submission and be in touch within 30 days if you're selected for a feature. We appreciate your patience — and your story.
