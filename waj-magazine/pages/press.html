ABOUT THE BRAND

WAJ Magazine is an independent media company founded in 2014 to spotlight the creatives, entrepreneurs, athletes, and community leaders that mainstream media overlooks. We operate at the intersection of journalism, entertainment, and community — telling stories that matter to the people who live them.

Our audience is diverse, passionate, and engaged: artists, musicians, business owners, fashion lovers, sports fans, and culture enthusiasts from across the United States and internationally.

We've covered everyone from up-and-coming New Jersey talents to nationally recognized names — always with the same commitment to authentic, community-driven storytelling.
